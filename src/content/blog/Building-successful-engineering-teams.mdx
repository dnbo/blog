Product obsession is a waste.
"Product-obsessed" might sound cool and sexy, but it's a bad culture. Refers to companies where all engineering is driven ONLY by product team who don't have crap about quality, only quantity. Doint the next thing, never fixing things (no visible value). Releasing a lot, fast and without paying attention to how it impacts the team and technology stack.

Obsession is never a good thing :)

Works both ways. Example - where engineering rules the world and is so obsessed with perfection that it leads to killing companies, especially in the startup environment.
